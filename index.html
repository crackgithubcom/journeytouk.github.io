在机场：

  • Getting off the plane: "Excuse me, where is the baggage claim area?"
  • 在移民处： "Could you please show me the way to immigration?"
  • 询问信息： "Where can I find the tourist information desk?"

打车或使用交通工具：

  • 打车： "Can you take me to [目的地], please?"
  • 地铁/公交： "Which platform does the [线路] train/bus leave from?"
  • 问司机： "Could you drop me off at [目的地], please?"

购物：

  • 在商店： "Excuse me, how much is this [商品]?"
  • 试衣服： "May I try this on, please?"
  • 讨价还价： "Is the price negotiable?"

用餐：

  • 餐厅预订： "Do I need a reservation?"
  • 点菜： "I'd like to order [菜名], please."
  • 付账： "Could we have the bill, please?"

问路：

  • "Excuse me, can you tell me how to get to [地点]?"
  • "Is it within walking distance?"
  • "Which direction is [地标]?"

使用Uber或打车应用：

  • "I would like to book a ride to [目的地], please."
  • "How long will it take to get there?"
  • "Could you please drop me off at [位置]?"

在旅馆/酒店：

  • 办理入住手续： "I have a reservation under [你的名字]."
  • 房间设施： "Is breakfast included?"
  • 询问服务： "Could you please send someone to clean the room?"
  • 结账离店： "What time is check-out?"

紧急情况：

  • "I need help. Can you please call the police/ambulance?"
  • "Is there a hospital near here?"
  • "I've lost my [物品]. Can you help me find it?"
